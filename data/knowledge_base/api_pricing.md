# API Pricing

## Pricing Tiers

Our API service offers three pricing tiers designed to meet different usage needs and budgets. Each tier provides access to our full suite of API endpoints with varying rate limits and support levels.

## Free Tier

The Free Tier is perfect for developers who want to explore our API capabilities without any financial commitment.

- **Price**: $0/month
- **Requests**: 1,000 requests per month
- **Rate Limit**: 10 requests per minute
- **Features**: Access to all basic endpoints
- **Support**: Community forum support
- **Data Retention**: 30 days

The Free Tier is ideal for hobby projects, proof of concepts, and initial development. No credit card is required to get started.

## Starter Tier

The Starter Tier is designed for small businesses and growing applications that need more than the free tier offers.

- **Price**: $29/month
- **Requests**: 50,000 requests per month
- **Rate Limit**: 100 requests per minute
- **Features**: All Free Tier features plus advanced analytics
- **Support**: Email support with 24-hour response time
- **Data Retention**: 90 days
- **Webhooks**: Up to 5 webhook endpoints

The Starter Tier includes access to our analytics dashboard, allowing you to monitor your API usage and performance in real-time.

## Professional Tier

The Professional Tier is our most popular plan for businesses with significant API usage requirements.

- **Price**: $99/month
- **Requests**: 500,000 requests per month
- **Rate Limit**: 500 requests per minute
- **Features**: All Starter Tier features plus priority processing
- **Support**: Priority email and chat support with 4-hour response time
- **Data Retention**: 1 year
- **Webhooks**: Unlimited webhook endpoints
- **Custom Integrations**: Dedicated integration support

Professional Tier customers receive priority processing, ensuring your requests are handled with minimal latency even during peak usage periods.

## Enterprise Tier

For organizations with high-volume requirements, we offer custom Enterprise solutions tailored to your specific needs.

- **Price**: Custom pricing based on usage
- **Requests**: Unlimited
- **Rate Limit**: Custom limits based on infrastructure
- **Features**: All Professional Tier features plus dedicated infrastructure
- **Support**: 24/7 phone and email support with dedicated account manager
- **Data Retention**: Custom retention policies
- **SLA**: 99.9% uptime guarantee
- **Security**: Advanced security features and compliance certifications

Enterprise customers receive a dedicated account manager who works closely with your team to ensure optimal performance and integration.

## Overage Charges

If you exceed your monthly request limit, additional requests are billed at the following rates:

- Free Tier: $0.01 per additional request
- Starter Tier: $0.005 per additional request
- Professional Tier: $0.002 per additional request

Enterprise plans include overage protection with custom pricing structures.

## Billing Cycle

All paid tiers are billed monthly. You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time, and changes will take effect at the start of the next billing cycle. Prorated credits are not provided for mid-cycle changes.

## Payment Methods

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express) and PayPal. Enterprise customers can also pay via invoice with NET 30 terms.

## Getting Started

To get started with any of our pricing tiers, simply sign up for an account on our developer portal. You'll receive an API key immediately and can begin making requests right away.
